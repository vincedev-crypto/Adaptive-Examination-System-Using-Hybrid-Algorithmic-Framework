Examination Management

Four Options:
1. Single PDF/Word: Questions with "Answer: ..." lines included
2. Two files: Upload questions + separate answer key (PDF, Word, or CSV)
3. CSV Format: Upload CSV file with columns: Question, ChoiceA, ChoiceB, ChoiceC, ChoiceD, Answer
4. Word (.docx): Upload Word document with native equations and images
📐 Mathematical Equations:
• Word (.docx): Native Word equations (built with Insert → Equation) are automatically converted to LaTeX
• Word images: Equation images embedded in Word are also extracted and displayed
• Inline math (PDF): wrap in $...$ → e.g. $x^2 + 3x = 0$
• Display math (PDF): wrap in $$...$$ → e.g. $$\frac{a}{b}=c$$
• Unicode symbols (², √, π, ≤, α, ∫, etc.) are automatically converted to LaTeX
